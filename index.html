Deep Learning based voice aging / de-aging

Additional content for the internship Speech transformation using deep generative models, conducted by A. CAILLON, supervised by A. Ozerov, N. Duong, G. Puy

Four models have been proposed during this internship to achieve voice age conversion, namely CGVC RND, CGVC TSS, VQVAE RND, VQVAE TSS. For confidentiality reasons, we can't explicitely define what those models are made of.
